# 对数放缩
$$\large \begin{align}
H(X)-\log_an&=\log_{a}e\sum_{i}p(a_i)\ln \dfrac{1}{np(a_i)}\\
&\leq\log_ae\sum_ip(a_i)\left(\dfrac{1}{np(a_i)}-1\right)\\
&=\log_ae\sum_i(\dfrac{1}{n}-p(a_i))\\
&=\log_ae\cdot(n-1)=0
\end{align}$$
即
$$\large H(X)\leq\log_an\quad当且仅当p(a_1)=\cdots=p(a_n)取等$$
# Jensen不等式
考察函数$f(x)=-x\log_a x(a>1)$
$$\large f''(x)=-\dfrac{1}{x\ln a}<0$$
故$f(x)$为上凸函数，有
$$\large f\left(\dfrac{1}{n}\sum_ix_i\right)\geq\dfrac{1}{n}\sum_if(x_i)$$
将$x_i=p(a_i)$代入有
$$\large -\dfrac{1}{n}\log_a\dfrac{1}{n}\geq-\dfrac{1}{n}\sum_ip(a_i)\log_ap(a_i)$$
整理得
$$\large H(X)\leq\log_an$$
