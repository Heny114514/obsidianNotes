# 二项展开
$$\large (a+b)^2=a^2+2ab+b^2$$
$$\large (a+b)^3=a^2+3a^2b+3ab^2+b^3$$
**例子**
$$\large (x\pm\dfrac{1}{x})^2$$
# 平方差、立方和、立方差
$$\large a^2-b^2=(a+b)(a-b)$$
$$\large a^3+b^3=(a+b)(a^2-ab+b^2)$$
$$\large a^3-b^3=(a-b)(a^2+ab+b^2)$$
**例子**
$$\large x^2-\dfrac{1}{x^2}$$
# 十字相乘
**基本形式**
$$\large ax^2+bx+c=(Ax+B)(Cx+D)=a(x-x_1)(x-x_2)$$
**要点**
- 展开、对比系数有
$$\large AC=a,BD=c,AD+BC=b$$
拆$a,c$，凑$b$

- 韦达定理
$$\large x_1x_2=\dfrac{c}{a},x_1+x_2=-\dfrac{b}{a}$$
**例子**
$$\large x^2−8x+15$$
$$\large 2x^2-7x-4$$
$$\large (5x+7)(3x-9)=15x^2-24x-63$$
$$\large x^2+y^2-2xy-3x+3y$$
$$\large x^2-\dfrac{a^2+1}{a}x+1$$
$$\large e^{2x}-(2^n+4)e^x+2^{n+2}$$
$$\large x^2+2xy-3y^2-5x-7y+6$$
# 根式套根式
**基本形式**
$$\large \sqrt{a+b\sqrt{c}}$$
**证明**
假设存在有理数$x,y$使得
$$\large \sqrt{x}+\sqrt{y}=\sqrt{a+b\sqrt{c}}$$
即
$$\large x+y+2\sqrt{xy}=a+b\sqrt{c}$$
寻找$x,y$满足
$$\large x+y=a,xy=\dfrac{b^2c}{4}$$
**例子**
$$\large \sqrt{5-2\sqrt{6}},\sqrt{4-\sqrt{15}}$$
